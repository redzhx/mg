本机启用ssh自动登陆

Last updated Aug 20, 2022 Edit Source

    1. 本地生成 RSA 密钥, 参考: https://www.jianshu.com/p/09b81c07e03f
    2. 输入 (多个用户登录输入多次):
    1

    ssh-copy-id -i ~/.ssh/id_rsa.pub username@192.168.2.22
